Registration

We encourage you to register for ICSME 16 and co-located events through the RegOnline event registration portal. On-site registration is also available at the late registration rate.

Early registration deadline is August 19, 2016. Please contact icsme16reg@gmail.com for questions or problems related to registration.

Note to Authors

Each accepted paper must have at least one author registered at a professional member rate. Papers for which the only registered authors are students must have one of the students register at the full (i.e., non-student) rate.

For a student to register at the full rate, during registration enter the Special Code FULLREG. This will allow the student to register at the full rate for ICSME and additionally register at regular student rates for other events.
