Social Events

Downtown Raleigh
Tuesday, October 4
5:00 PM EDT–7:15 PM EDT
Total ride time: 30 minutes
Ride intensity: Low

Raleigh has over 100 miles of greenways to explore! Explore a (small) part of them during this 30 minute bike ride ending at a local Raleigh brewery. Enjoy their famous 40 oz. tasting flight, hang out and play corn hole, and grab some grub from the food truck before cycling back to the hotel.

Raleigh Marriott City Center
State Ballroom
Wednesday, October 5
6:30 PM EDT–10:00 PM EDT

The conference reception will be held in the conference venue and will feature a relaxed atmosphere with food, drinks, and sport. Experience true Southern living as you play Cornhole while drinking local craft brew. Participate in the Cornhole tournament for a chance to win fame, friends, and an ugly commemorative medal.

Centennial Fields
Upper Field
Friday, October 7
5:30 PM EDT–7:00 PM EDT

The soccer game will be held on a natural grass field on the Centennial Campus of NC State University immediately after the conference ends. The game bus will leave from the Raleigh Marriott City Center at 5:30 PM EDT and return to the hotel at 7:00 PM EDT.
