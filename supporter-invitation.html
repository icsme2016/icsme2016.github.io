Invitation to Support ICSME 2016

ICSME is the premier international venue in software maintenance and evolution, where researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government have met annually for the past 31 years. The 2016 conference will be held in downtown Raleigh and will be attended by over 250 scientists and software engineers from all over the world — including many students.

Corporate contributions are key to the success of conferences such as ICSME 2016. We invite you to support the conference as a corporate benefactor. Benefits and prices are listed in the table below.

Additional benefits or support levels may be negotiated. Please contact Nicholas A. Kraft, General Co-chair at nicholas.a.kraft@us.abb.com or +1 919 856 2456 with questions or ideas.
